Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Commitment

The Factorio Calculator is built with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe your factory planning should be completely private and secure.

Data Collection & Storage

We are committed to transparency about our data practices:

  • No personal information is ever collected
  • No user tracking or analytics are implemented
  • No cookies are used on our website
  • All calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No user inputs are saved or transmitted

Third-Party Services

The Factorio Calculator operates entirely within your browser:

  • We do not use any third-party analytics services
  • We do not integrate with any tracking tools
  • We do not serve any advertisements
  • We do not use any external marketing services

Note: The Factorio Calculator is designed to be a simple, privacy-respecting tool for the Factorio community. We believe that factory planning tools should not compromise user privacy, which is why we've made the conscious decision to avoid any form of data collection or tracking.
